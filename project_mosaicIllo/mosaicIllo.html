Mosaic Illustration Branding

During my internship at TeleTracking Technologies, in addition to my primary project of designing the Mosaic design system, I took on the project of making illustration branding for Mosaic. I was asked to create illustrations that presented Mosaic in an accessible way because the team wanted to present on Mosaic at the upcoming client conference.

MY ROLE

Illustrator

DURATION

the last few weeks of my internship; my internship was then extended for another 2 weeks.

SKILLS

Sketching, Illustration, Animation

TOOLS

Principle, Illustrator

Mosaic Animation

I first created an animation of the Mosaic logo that could be used at the client conference. The idea for the animation was first sketched out by another designer. I used Principle to animate according to his sketches and iterated on it several times with another intern. This is the final product.

Style Explorations

I drew from the existing branding including the Mosaic logo and an animation of the logo I previously created. The existing branding is very playful. The animation is very bouncy and all of the shapes morph from a circle. For the illustration branding, I made the figures very round and playful and also incorporated some transparency to mimic the logo.

I came up with four different styles, and I asked the design team for feedback. We settled on the 4th style.
